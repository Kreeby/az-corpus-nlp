It is impossible not to fall in love with Icherisheher.
This unique place has witnessed glorious events in the past which transform it into the romantic 
setting of the present day.
Icherisheher is an ancient city that never exposes itself fully. It seems to contemplate its own 
fascinating history and live in its own rhythm, making it impossible to resist for anyone who enters the 
city.
Icherisheher, known as the Inner City or Old Town, is the jewel in the crown of the cultural heritage of 
the Azerbaijani people and a source of pride of every Azerbaijani. It is a unique town within the 
modern city of Baku. While this unique World Heritage Site is a living town with its own infrastructure 
and residential dwellings, it also contains hundreds of monuments of local, national and world 
importance and sits on 22 hectares.
Icherisheher is a town of magnificent medieval palaces and mosques, caravanserais and squares, 
picturesque streets and quiet parks. A visitor can enjoy numerous cultural monuments and wander 
around quiet streets where time has seemingly stopped. Visitors can also browse through various 
souvenir shops and craftsman's workshops, relax in cozy cafes and enjoy Azeri national cuisine in 
traditional restaurants.
